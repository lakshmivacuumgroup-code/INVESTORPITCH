Mr. L. N. Prasad

Mr. L. N. Prasad is a technocrat and visionary electrical engineer with over two decades of experience in vacuum heat treatment technology. He founded the business in 2003, when vacuum furnace technology in India was dominated by MNCs.

With a focus on indigenization and cost-effective innovation, he pioneered indigenous vacuum furnace technology, positioning Lakshmi Vacuum Group as an early mover in a high-barrier segment.
