Executive Summary

Lakshmi Vacuum Group (LVTPL) is a technology-driven engineering company specializing in vacuum heat treatment systems and allied industrial solutions, established in 2003.

A strong indigenous player in a niche segment historically dominated by global manufacturers. Diversified business model combining capital equipment manufacturing and recurring service revenues demonstrates strong scalability and long-term growth visibility.
